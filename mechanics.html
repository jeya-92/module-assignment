Quantum Computing:
 Explore the potential of quantum computers to solve problems that are intractable for classical computers. Projects could focus on specific quantum algorithms, the design of quantum gates, or the challenges of building and scaling quantum computers.
Quantum Cryptography:
 Investigate how quantum mechanics can be used to create secure communication channels. Projects could explore quantum key distribution protocols, the security of quantum cryptographic systems, or the development of quantum sensors.
Quantum Sensors:
 Explore the use of quantum phenomena to build highly sensitive sensors for various applications. Projects could focus on the design and fabrication of quantum sensors for measuring magnetic fields, temperature, or other physical quantities.
Quantum Optics:
 Explore the interaction of light and matter at the quantum level. Projects could involve studying lasers, optical amplifiers, or the use of quantum optics in quantum computing and communication.
